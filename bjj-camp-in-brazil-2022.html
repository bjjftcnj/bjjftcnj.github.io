BJJ Camp in Brazil with Professor Marcelo Oliveira - October 9th to 16th, 2022

BJJ CAMP IN BRAZIL
Belo Horizonte and Morro de São Paulo
October 9th to October 16, 2022
Belo Horizonte, Brazil

Professor Marcelo Oliveira from the Jiu-Jitsu Family Training Center in Edgewater, New Jersey, is offering you a chance to experience authentic Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu training in Belo Horizonte, Brazil.

Exceptional world champions were built in Belo Horizonte’s Jiu-Jitsu schools, such as Felipe Pena, Romulo Barral, Samuel Braga, Paulo Curi, Christiano Lazzarini, Sergio Benini, Cláudio Caloquinha, Rafael Natal, and many more. This is the city where Professor Marcelo Oliveira, 5th degree Black Belt in BJJ and former MMA fighter, grew up learning a variety of martial arts and started his jiu-jitsu training.

Now, he wants to share his experience with you!

Professor Marcelo Oliveira has arranged groups sessions at several world-famous academies in Belo Horizonte, including Felipe Pena Academy, Peposo Academy, and Gracie Barra Buritis - BH Academy, as well as a visit to the Equipe de Lutas Bocao Team. You will attend sessions lead by top instructors and have a chance to roll with local BJJ students of all levels.

After your training sessions, you will have free time to tour the city, enjoy hotel pool, or to visit Tiro Urbano Israeli Defensive School. Learn how Belo Horizonte's local law enforcement officers train in personal security and firearms. The security personnel we hire for this camp are former/retired police officers and were all trained at the Tiro Urbano Shooting School.

Morro de São Paulo, Brazil

After our visit to Belo Horizonte, we will travel to Salvador Bahia State to Morro de São Paulo, one of the most beautiful and safest destinations in Brazil. It’s home to palm-fringed beaches and is lined with stores and restaurants. After a flight to Salvador, we will travel by catamaran from Salvador to Morro de Sao Paulo and experience the beauty of the Brazilian coast.

In Morro de São Paulo, you will stay at the highly-rated Hotel Portaló. With a beautiful sea view and an unforgettable sunset, Hotel Portaló offers its guests cozy chalets, apartments and suites, as well as a panoramic pool overlooking the ocean.

In the hotel, Professor Marcelo Oliveira will conduct 2-hour drilling sessions and review everything that was taught at the other academies. You will have an intense jiu-jitsu training session in the morning and relax on the gorgeous beaches of Morro de São Paulo in the afternoon. You also have the opportunity to visit the splendid village, eat lunch at local restaurants, or spend the afternoon in the hotel pool.

On our last day, we will take a private boat to Boipeba Island, one of the islands of the Tinharé archipelago. Surrounded by the ocean on one side, and the Inferno River estuary on the other, this island is of rare natural beauty, home to a great variety of ecosystems. The island has been recognized by UNESCO as a Biosphere Reserve and as a World Heritage Site.
