BJJ CAMP IN BRAZIL
Belo Horizonte and Morro de São Paulo
October 9th to October 16, 2022
Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu Training in Belo Horizonte

The Jiu-Jitsu Family Training Center is offering you a chance to experience “arte suave” in the heart of where it all started - Belo Horizonte, Brazil. Exceptional world champions were built in Belo Horizonte’s Jiu-Jitsu schools, such as Felipe Pena, Romulo Barral, Samuel Braga, Paulo Curi, Christiano Lazzarini, Sergio Benini, Cláudio Caloquinha and many more. This is the city where Professor Marcelo Oliveira grew up learning a variety of martial arts and where he started his Jiu-Jitsu training.

Now, he wants to share his experience with you!

You’ll have a chance to train at several world-famous academies in Belo Horizonte. We have arranged private group sessions at Felipe Pena Academy, Peposo Academy, and Gracie Barra Buritis - BH Academy, as well as a visit to the Equipe de Lutas Bocao Team.

Morro de São Paulo & Boipeba Island

Along with intense jiu-jitsu training sessions with world-class instructors in Belo Horizonte, we will travel to Salvador Bahia State to visit Morro de São Paulo, one of the most beautiful and safest destinations in Brazil. It’s home to palm-fringed beaches and is lined with stores and restaurants. After a flight to Salvador, we will travel by catamaran from Salvador to Morro de Sao Paulo and experience the beauty of the Brazilian coast.

Train in the morning with Professor Marcelo Oliveira and relax on the beach in the afternoon.

On our last day, we will take a private boat to Boipeba Island, one of the islands of the Tinharé archipelago. Surrounded by the ocean on one side, and the Inferno River estuary on the other, this island is of rare natural beauty, home to a great variety of ecosystems. The island has been recognized by UNESCO as a Biosphere Reserve and as a World Heritage Site.
